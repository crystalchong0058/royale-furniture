Who are We?

Inspire. Learn.

Our company is created with love and inspiration. With a distinct architectural aesthetic, experiential store environment, and online community, we engage with our customers through social media, mobile shopping, design services, gift registry, and more.

Our Story

Founded by Gordon and Carole Segal in 1962, Royale Furniture was born after a European honeymoon sparked the idea to bring simple, beautiful and practical housewares to the US market. Their passion resulted in our first storefront, which used crates and barrels as display fixtures in an old elevator factory on Chicagoâ€™s Wells Street. Today, we greet over 22 million customers in our stores each year and welcome more than 100 million visitors online.

Our Accomplishments

We are a gifting destination and passionate about customer service, a combination that has brought more than 2 million couples to our wedding registry alone. In 2001, Royale Furniture acquired The Land of Nod, a brand offering kids furniture and toys. In 2018, they became Royaleekids, the newest (and tiniest) member of Crate and Barrel, providing customers the opportunity to shop with us for even more of the moments that matter in their lives.
