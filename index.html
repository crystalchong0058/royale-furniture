Our Mission

A curated and crafted experience that connects design with the individuals who seek it. Founded in the Pacific Northwest in 2011, our company grew from a desire to modernize the furniture industry. Focusing on quality and contemporary design, we offer sophisticated home decor to help create your inspired lifestyle. What separates us from others is our sheer devotion to craft. We have distribution center facilities throughout the United States and Canada. Our facility in Tracy, California was the largest distribution center to become LEED GOLD certified in the United States. Our lifestyle brands offer inspired living through high-quality products, exclusive designs, and timeless style - all powered by digital design and visualization tools that provide seamless shopping solutions in-store and online.
